Values
A brand to be proud of.
Every Yoru holder should be proud and glad to show off what they have, and be proud to be a representative of Yoru.
Providing value to holders.
Every Yoru holder should be provided more value, both in and outside the Solana ecosystem, than they put into Yoru.
A community second to none.
Like none other. A community of inventors and innovators, traders and degens, vibe maxis and the dignified alike.
Delivery > Promises.
In your time, you’ll be a promised a million Xs and a billion Ys. But how many will you actually receive? Yoru sticks to the principle of no games - just delivering.
